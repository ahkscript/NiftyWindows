Here you can download the latest version of NiftyWindows. You have to decide wheter you prefer the automatic installer (highly recommended to ease further updates) or the pure zipped archive for manual extraction. The release package includes only the main executable and some additional text files.

Note: Redistribution of NiftyWindows and linking to the website are welcome and strongly encouraged. In other words, feel free to share this tool with the public, provided that the enclosed license is met.

Look out for future pre-releases of NiftyWindows here also. There are public pre-releases of upcoming minor versions at regular intervals. These versions provide the most current features for testing and demonstration purposes only. They may be unstable and there will be further updates to the same minor version number. You can check the given build.txt (inside the archive) to track the build date.
